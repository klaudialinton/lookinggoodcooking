ABOUT LOOKING GOOD COOKING

Looking Good Cooking (LGC) was opened on 2nd January 2020 with the goal of serving some local and western cuisines. With these setting of foods in the menu, its will makes our guests can tastes the deliciousness of the local and western meals. The ideas of the menus came from when Johnny went to Italy for his holiday trip, even though he was there for a week, he suddenly missed the local cuisines. So, after he went back home to Malaysia, Johnny got an idea to make a small business of cuisine restaurant. He put on local and western cuisine on the LGC menu.
